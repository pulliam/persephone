WELCOME TO CODE BREAKER GAME.

INTRUCTIONS:

  • Click Right with your cursor on the page
  • Select'Inspect Element'
  • In the window that opens inside this page, press the button "Console"
  • Type breakCode('TRYAPASSWORD') in the console and replace the word TRYAPASSWORD inside quotation marks for a password of your choice, then press enter
  • Your goal is to break into the password, by guessing it right. Everytime one of the characters of your password is right, we will let you know so you can keep trying until you get it right!

Good Luck!
