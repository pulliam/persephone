How to cook spicy salmon with bok choy

Or how to cross a river with your whole family and a terrible boat

  1. Gather the following ingredients:
    • 1 cup long-grain white rice
    • 2 tablespoons honey
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
    • 4 6-ounce skinless salmon fillets
    • 1 pound baby bok choy
  2. Cook the rice according to the package directions.
  3. Meanwhile, combine the honey, soy sauce, and red pepper.
  4. On a foil-lined rimmed baking sheet, broil the salmon fillets until opaque throughout, 8 to 10 minutes, basting with the honey mixture during the last 3 minutes.
  5. Steam baby bok choy (cut into quarters) until tender, 8 to 10 minutes.
  6. Serve with the salmon and rice.
